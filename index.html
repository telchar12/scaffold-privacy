Privacy Policy

Scaffold · Last updated: July 2026

Summary

  • Your health data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • When you use AI features, your profile and health context is sent to the AI provider you select.
  • We (the app developers) do not operate servers that store your health logs. Optional services (crash reports, Google Sign-In, Drive backup) are described below.
  • You can use the app fully without AI — manual entry works without sending data to an AI provider.
  • Version 1 does not include voice input, Apple Health, or Google Health Connect sync.
  • The free tier may show non-personalized ads. A one-time lifetime purchase removes ads permanently. Health logs and profile data are never used to target ads.

Data Storage

Local-only data

Stays on your device unless you export, use AI, enable crash reports, or use optional Google backup:

  • Meal, exercise, and sleep entries; daily rollups
  • Chat history; user profile; app settings
  • AI provider API keys (encrypted device storage; not in JSON exports)

Data sent to AI providers

When you use AI features, the text or image you provide and your profile summary may be sent to the provider you configured. Manual entry avoids this. Update or remove profile data anytime in Settings → Profile.

Advertising (free tier)

If you have not purchased lifetime ad-free access, the app may show non-personalized ads through Google AdMob. Ads are not chosen from your health logs. Where required (for example EEA/UK), a consent message may appear before ads.

Ad-free: a one-time lifetime in-app purchase removes ads; after purchase the ad SDK is not used to show ads.

In-app purchases

The lifetime ad-free product is sold through Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). Payment is processed by the store. We may receive a purchase receipt/token to unlock and restore ad-free access on your devices.

Your rights

  • View, export (JSON/CSV), or Drive-back up your data
  • Delete local data by uninstalling (Drive backups remain in your Google account until you delete them)
  • Stop AI sharing by not using AI or clearing your API key
  • Disable crash reporting in Settings
  • Remove ads via lifetime purchase or Restore purchases
